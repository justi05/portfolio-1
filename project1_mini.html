WildCam

Smart Monitoring System to Mitigate Human-Wildlife Conflict

About Project

This project focused on developing a smart monitoring system to effectively mitigate human-wildlife conflict. The project aimed to address the persistent issue of wild animal intrusion in residential and agricultural areas, which poses significant challenges for farmers and rural communities. By utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms and innovative technology, we designed a system capable of detecting the presence of wild animals. Our goal is to provide an efficient solution that empowers farmers to protect their livelihoods, ensures food security, and fosters a harmonious coexistence between humans and wildlife.

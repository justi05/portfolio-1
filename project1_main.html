Glucose Creatinine Monitoring System


About Project

Ongoing research focusing on creating a groundbreaking point-of-care device that prioritizes monitoring glucose and creatinine levels. The primary goal is to transform healthcare by providing accurate creatinine monitoring for early identification of kidney diseases and proactive glucose monitoring for diabetes care.
This initiative highlights biomedical advancements, emphasizing innovation in point-of-care diagnostics to improve patient care and promote proactive health management.
